Basiima Kabunga Heron Felix
Director · Nze KB Arts Marketing Agency & Kabalega Rise Media
Who is Basiima Kabunga Heron Felix? Basiima Kabunga Heron Felix is a Ugandan Journalist, Media Professional, and Creative Entrepreneur. He is the Director of Nze KB Arts Marketing Agency and Kabalega Rise Media, with over 8 years of experience in Media, Branding, and Marketing. Throughout his career, Basiima has worked with more than 10 corporate organizations, helping to promote brands and products through Photography, Graphics design, Media Coverage, and Digital Marketing. In 2025, he gained International Exposure when he covered Team Uganda at the Under-15 Volleyball Championship in Shangluo, China. In 2024, under Nze KB Arts, he won two awards at the Kitara Music Generation Awards: • Best Photographer of the Year • Best Graphics Designer of the Year He has worked with several organizations including: Uganda Secondary Schools Sports Association (USSSA), USSSA TV, Uganda Revenue Authority (Tax Academy), Bunyoro-Kitara Kingdom, FUFA, Kabalega Secondary School, Kyobe Frontiers Uganda Ltd, Stumut Engineering Limited, Boma FC (PR), and Buhimba United FC. Basiima continues to use media and marketing to promote brands, sports, and organizations across Uganda and beyond.
